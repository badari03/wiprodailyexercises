Designing with Simplicity

By Badarinath | August 12, 2025

Welcome to a journey through clean design principles. In this post, we explore how minimalism enhances readability and user experience.

Section One: Typography

Choosing the right font family and size is crucial. Sans-serif fonts like Segoe UI or Roboto offer clarity and modern aesthetics.

Section Two: Layout

Using Flexbox and Grid allows for responsive, structured layouts that adapt to any screen size.

Section Three: Color Palette

Limit your palette to 3–4 colors. This keeps the design cohesive and prevents visual overload.
